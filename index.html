Welcome to Animegrimoire


We're sharing LQ bit-starved 720p anime encodes (about 100MB per episodes) from Ongoing season and occasionally BD Releases.
Re-encoded using Handbrake with animegrimoire's preset.

Why bitstarved encodes? - because you could find "Best" encodes anywhere. we're in niche part where we don't care that much for visual quality and we're looking to save our storage space.

This project runs as personal hobby, we are not promising 100% uptime activity, we may continuously encodes during our free time or idle all day long when we're busy

Animegrimoire is maintained by Aurora Team in memorial cyber12.com (2006-2016) and animegrimoire.org (2016-2020). We've always loved you!
